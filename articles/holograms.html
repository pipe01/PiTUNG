Holograms

Holograms are pieces of text that float on your screen, tracking a point in the world. They can either track a fixed point or a GameObject. For this, the PiTung.Mod_utilities.Hologram class has two constructors: Hologram(string, Vector3) and Hologram(string, GameObject). The first one accepts a Vector3, representing the point in the world that we want to track, and the second one accepts a game object that it will track.
